The system is designed to improve workflow visibility, reduce downstream verification debt, and support reproducible research operations.

## CV Version: Business Economics / Applied Micro

Developed a modular research support system for structured source intake, validation, review routing, and downstream analysis preparation. The system emphasizes human oversight, explicit quality gates, and reproducible workflow design rather than fully automated research claims.

## Outreach Version (80-120 words)

This repository is a sanitized showcase of a broader non-public research automation environment. It demonstrates a review-gated, human-in-the-loop research system for literature, link, and document intake, with modular routing, validation, structured review packets, and downstream export support. The public version is intentionally compact: it uses toy inputs and clean sample outputs to illustrate how uncertain artifacts are paused for review rather than passed silently into later stages. The goal is to show serious systems design for research operations, including progress visibility, structured outputs, and reproducibility-minded architecture, without exposing unpublished materials or non-public working files.
